About Expenses

Expenses help you track where your money goes. By recording every purchase or payment, you can better understand your spending habits and manage your finances effectively.

How to Use

  • Add a description of the expense.
  • Select the category that best fits the purchase.
  • Enter the amount and the date of the transaction.
  • The expense will appear in your dashboard and cashflow records.
